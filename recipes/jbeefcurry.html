Japanese Beef Curry

What is Japanese beef curry?

Made with beef, potatoes, carrots, and golden curry curry blocks, and a secret ingredient. This Japanese beef curry is going to make your taste buds come back for more helpings than you could reasonably eat.

Ingredients and Equipment:

  • Golden Curry Beef Curry Blocks
  • 3 Carrots
  • 2 Onions
  • 2 Potatoes
  • 1 Fuji Apple (or apple of choice)
  • 1 lb. of Beef (don't forget this unless you're vegan)
  • 2 tbsp. on Olive Oil
  • About 4 cups of water (read curry block box)
  • 1 Kitchen cutting knife
  • 1 Wok
  • 1 boiling pot

Steps:

  1. Fill boiling pot with required amount of water and begin to boil.
  2. Cut onions into small chunks and begin sauteing in the wok with 2 tbsp. of olive oil.
  3. While the onions are sauteing, cut down the carrots and potatoes into desired size.
  4. Add vegetables to wok and cook until onions are browning.
  5. Once the vegetables are cooked add them to the boiling pot, let simmer.
  6. While the vegetables are simmering, throw the beef into the wok, begin to brown the beef.
  7. Once the beef is browned add the beef to the boiling pot of vegetables.
  8. Let the pot simmer on low heat for about 30 minutes.
  9. Once 30 minutes is up then add the curry blocks to the mixture.
  10. Stir occasionally but let simmer for about 10 minutes.
  11. While the curry is simmering finely shred your apple of choice and add it to the pot.
  12. Eat!
